title: "Access your XA Hosting Game Server"
icon: "rocket-launch"
---

### 1. **Log In to the Game Panel.**
    - To log into Panel, please visit **https://panel.xahosting.co/**.
    - Use the same credentials as [Billing](https://billing.xahosting.co/dashboard) (username/email and password).

### 2. **Find you server.**
    - Once logged in, click on your server to access its dashboard.
